About

Language learning happens everywhere.

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in Applied Psychology at New York University, working with Catherine Tamis-LeMonda. I earned my Ph.D. in Communicative Sciences and Disorders at NYU under Sudha Arunachalam.

My work brings together experimental methods, naturalistic observation, and language-sample analysis to understand learning in monolingual and bilingual children.
